The Everfrost

Government: None
Current Leader: None
Goal: Just chilling, man, y'know?

Section One: Geography

Snow :3

Section Two: Culture

There are humans native to this region that live in small tribes of various cultures from around Noiful-Planeth, all blended together, much like in The Bricklands. There are also local cultures of humans who originally are from The Everfrost. They make complex pelts with ornate designs, and make goggles out of bones of animals to shield their eyes from becoming snowblinded. They hardly have any weapons, save for fishing spears, and rarely spears designed for combat, as they believe in peace. There are also Arcanians who are native to The Everfrost, those who find the smell of fear pleasant, and those who find the smell of fear repulsive alike.

Section Three: Significance

The Everfrost is the edge of Noiful-Planeth. There is nothing beyond The Everfrost save for The Void, which I won't speak of here.
