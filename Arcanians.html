Arcanians

Decendant of: Rats
Populations in:
The Bricklands,
Tche'et,

Section One: The Arcanian Condition

The Arcanian Condition is extremely simple. When Arcanians enter puberty, they develop a sense of smell for fear. At this point, they either grow to love the smell or hate it. Those who hate the smell are the kindest, most empathetic beings you would ever meet. Those who love the smell are the most evil, vile, torturous beings in existence.

Section Two: Appearance

Arcanians appear as bipedal, 3-5 foot tall rats. They have the same body type regardless of biological sex. They have no thumbs, but instead have a digit that faces the opposite way of the other two, allowing for tool usage.

Section Three: Significance

The Arcanians are the most magically atuned beings on the planet. They are known to be wizards (usually), and have many wizard spires in The Bricklands, Tche'et, and Squee-et alike. Many are also advisors to the Philosopher King in Ellen-et.
